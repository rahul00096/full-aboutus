Our Journey So Far, Not Always By Bus...

Dynamic Office Seating Limited is born - 2006

Having worked in the office furniture industry, Carl Verlander became aware of a unique selling point; to deliver office seating next day. Here the trio of Carl, Josh and George was born. Often working all night building chairs to deliver around the country, the vision of Dynamic soon became a reality.

2006
2007

Life began at a unit in Moulton Park - 2007

It wasn’t long before the support of others was needed, and the team grew to 7; Jess started her journey with us at this time and you may still speak to her to this very day. It was during this early stage that the “Make it Happen” belief was born and continues even now.

It became clear that a bigger building was needed: Gladstone Road - 2009

Here things started to snowball and with the addition of more department managers and some of our biggest customers joining us, we grew from strength to strength. Those customers are still with us today and are our biggest champions.

2009
2012

Then a bigger, bigger building: Ross Road - 2014

Ross Road sped the growth of Dynamic with the team increasing to over 70 employees and the range expanding to cover office furniture and office accessories. We introduced a 24-hour operation and continued to have year on year growth.

Dynamic Bus - Dynamibus - 2015

‘Dynamibus’ was born and gave us a mobile showroom, we can bring to you and demonstrate a sizable selection of our exciting products in a unique way. Our onboard specialist provides ‘Furnology’ training sessions to help educate and enhance knowledge and understanding.

2014
2015

Ross Road Showroom - 2016

An extensive makeover of our showroom, showcasing our wonderful products and delighting visitors every day. A design lead showroom, displaying our seating and furniture ranges, with our customers in mind.

TNT - Tuesday Night Team - 2017

We wanted to help those less fortunate and in need of some warm food and clothing, so our relationship with Tuesday Night Team was formed – a local charity helping and supporting the homeless in Northampton. We continue to support TNT to this day.

2016
2012

Mill Road Building - 2018

Ross Road became a squeeze and Mill Road was acquired to house our forever growing product offering; Dynamic vans whizzed between the two buildings making sure all orders went out of our door, to your door.

Virtual Showroom Visits - March 2021

‘Zoom’ and ‘Teams’ became verbs during the pandemic as most of us started working from home. whether that’s with colleagues, customers or suppliers. When we are organising meetings or events, we ask the people who are joining, if they’re happy to go virtual and make it easy for them to do so. This is a simple way to cut down on our business travel, slashing greenhouse gas emissions and saving time and money in the process. We still use this form of communication today.

WMS - Warehouse Management System - March 2021

Our Warehouse Management System was implemented to further grow the business and enhance our customer experience. The system automatically classifies all orders by size and selects the right courier to deliver, using the right vehicle by size. This ensures that we deliver product with least impact on the environment as possible. Getting it right first time

TreeNation - April 2022

Our mission is to reforest the world and every time you place an order, we plant a tree. Thanks to the Tree-Nation's reforestation and conservation projects we help to restore forests, create jobs, support local communities and protect biodiversity. Through the Tree-Nation platform we aim to bring a technological solution to the problem of Deforestation, responsible for about 17% of all Climate Change emissions. It’s as easy as 1,2, tree!

Real Living Wage - September 2022

We joined to ensure that our staff get the wage they deserve and which meets their every day needs. The real Living Wage rates are higher because they are independently-calculated based on what people need to get by. That's why we encourage all employers that can afford to do so to ensure their employees earn a wage that meets the costs of living, not just the government minimum. We are one of 11,000 businesses and encourage you to join too!
